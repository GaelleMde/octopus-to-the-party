🐙 About the game

Octopus to the party is a playful twist on Pulpo á Feira, a classic Galician dish. It’s the kind of funny mistranslation tourists — or guiris — might come up with. A fun tribute to Galician culture, with an octopus dodging trash!

🎯 Game Objective

  • Help Pulpiño swim to the party!
  • Goal: score as many points as possible.
  • Avoid the trash left behind by humans (because, let’s be honest, they’re pigs 🐷).
  • Every time you hit a piece of garbage → you lose 1 life ❤️.
  • You have 5 lives in total.

🎮 Controls

  • Use the arrow keys ⬅️⬆️➡️⬇️ to swim and dodge the obstacles and catch the shrimps 🦐
